Driving profile 300/750
Each day you will be allocated 25 Drive points. Maintain your daily Drive points balance by driving well during the day by accelerating, braking and cornering smoothly, avoiding speeding and avoiding cellphone use. Harsh driving events will reduce your daily 25 Drive points balance, depending on the type and severity of the driving event. At the end of every day, your remaining daily Drive points will bank towards your monthly Driving Profile, which is a build-up of your daily Drive points, up to 750 Vitality Drive points a month. This will contribute towards your monthly Vitality Drive points to earn up to 50% fuel cash back each month.
Night-time drivig 50/100
Avoid driving at night especially between 23:00 and 04:30. During these times, there is decreased visibility, it's harder to focus and there are more impaired drivers on the road.

Rather use our Drive Me partners and get up to 25% off Uber, Road Trip or Scooter Angels trips to get home safely.
Distance points 50/150
We look at the total number of kilometres you drove over the past 30 days. The more time spent on the road, the higher your risk of being in an accident.
Claim-free years 100/100
You can earn up to 100 Vitality Drive points a month by not claiming: earn 50 Vitality Drive points for 2 claim-free years. Earn 100 Vitality Drive points for 3 claim-free years. Vehicle glass or SOS claims won't affect your claim-free years.
